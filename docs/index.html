What it does

One thing, done without leaving your Mac.

Copy code, hit a hotkey, and Vitrine renders a polished image with your saved style — or open the editor when you want to fine-tune.

Capture

Lives in the menu bar with a global hotkey. Reads the clipboard and detects code, a URL, or HTML — then picks the renderer for you.

Web snapshots

Render pasted HTML to an image, or capture a webpage at one or more sizes — entirely locally in WebKit on your Mac, with a first-use privacy disclosure (direct-download build).

Style

13 themes plus your own, 160+ languages, developer fonts, gradient and image backgrounds, focus mode, and GitHub-style diff coloring.

Annotate

A CleanShot-style palette: arrows, boxes, text, a highlighter, blur/redaction, and numbered counters — drawn on the live preview.

Export & share

Retina PNG/PDF to clipboard, file, or the Share Sheet. OpenGraph, Instagram Story, and GitHub-banner presets, plus a vitrine CLI.

Private by design

Rendering is fully local and sandboxed — no account, no network by default, no telemetry. Your code never leaves your Mac.

Crafted

One design-token system across every surface, light and dark. English & Spanish, Sparkle auto-updates, Shortcuts and App Intents.
