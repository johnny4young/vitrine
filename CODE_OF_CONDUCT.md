# Vitrine Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in Vitrine welcoming, safe, and equitable for
everyone. We value respectful collaboration, honest technical discussion, and
constructive feedback from people of every background and experience level.

## Encouraged behavior

- Be kind, patient, and specific.
- Respect different viewpoints and experiences.
- Give and accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Take responsibility for mistakes and repair harm when possible.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its community.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, intimidation, or unwanted personal attention.
- Insults, personal attacks, discriminatory language, or sexualized content.
- Publishing another person's private information without permission.
- Deliberate disruption, impersonation, or repeated bad-faith participation.
- Any conduct that would reasonably make another contributor feel unsafe.

## Scope

This code applies in all Vitrine community spaces, including issues, pull
requests, reviews, and other places where someone represents the project.

## Reporting and enforcement

Report possible violations privately to the maintainer at
[asesordeprogramacion@gmail.com](mailto:asesordeprogramacion@gmail.com). Do not open
a public issue. Reports will be handled confidentially and reviewed promptly.

Project maintainers may edit or remove contributions, comments, and other content
that violates this code. Depending on severity and history, responses may include
a private warning, temporary limits on participation, or a permanent ban.
